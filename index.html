Sounds great, but who are you?

Hi, I’m Riccardo, UX/UI Designer based in Bristol, England.

I’m passionate about Visual Design and User Experience because it represents the perfect balance between creative and analytical thinking. I like the idea that what I do connects businesses and individuals through problem solving and design.

I’m also passionate about travelling, learning about different cultures, and ways of thinking. I also like tattoos, beer and lifting heavy things over my head, you can keep me interested by mentioning them in a conversation.

My skills involve:

  • UX/UI Design
  • Interaction Design
  • Visual Design
  • UX Research and Analytics
